Welcome to the Language Cafe

At the heart of our bustling city lies the Language Cafe, a cozy haven for language enthusiasts and cultural explorers alike. Here, we believe that the journey of learning a language is more than just mastering grammar and vocabulary; it's about embracing new cultures, fostering lasting friendships, and expanding our worldviews.

If you have any questions, this section is for you.

When and Where Are the Meetups Held?

  • Saturdays: Join us from 16:30 to 19:00 at Breitscheidstraße 2f, 70174 Stuttgart.
  • Alternate Thursdays: We gather every second Thursday from 19:00 to 21:30, at the same location.
  • Extra Saturday Session: Due to popular demand, there's a second meetup at Kulturinsel, Güterstraße 4, 70372 Stuttgart, at the same time as the regular Saturday session.

How does it work?

  • Preparation: Each week, a discussion topic with a list of questions is announced a week in advance to help attendees prepare and brush up on relevant vocabulary.
  • Group Dynamics: At the meetup, attendees are divided into small groups based on language proficiency, each led by a coordinator.
  • Role of the Coordinator: The coordinator, fluent in German, will assist with questions and ensure everyone gets a chance to contribute.
